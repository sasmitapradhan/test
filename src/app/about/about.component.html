About Us

GreenHance provides effective and unique solution for all kind of biodegradable waste into compost. We are actively encouraging people to stop dumping waste and rather utilize our solutions to keep our environment safe from poisonous liquid called "LEACHATE" formed by decomposing of organic waste. If this liquid mixes with surface and ground water, the whole water will be contaminated and get polluted. This way both environment and water gets polluted which can be avoided by using our simple solutions.
